Privacy Policy

FitSchedule ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the FitSchedule mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Storage

FitSchedule operates on a local-first, privacy-focused principle:

  • All data stays on your device: All information you enter into FitSchedule (including member names, contact information, session data, events, and schedules) is stored locally on your device only.
  • No cloud storage: We do not upload, sync, or store any of your data on external servers or cloud services.
  • No account required: FitSchedule does not require registration, login, or account creation.
  • No tracking: We do not track your usage, collect analytics, or monitor your behavior within the app.

2. Contact Access

FitSchedule requests access to your device contacts to allow you to import member information:

  • Contact access is optional and only used when you choose to import contacts.
  • Contact data is only read when you explicitly select contacts to import.
  • Imported contact information is stored locally on your device.
  • We never upload or share your contacts with any third party.

3. WhatsApp Integration

The App includes a feature to open WhatsApp conversations with your members:

  • This feature simply opens the WhatsApp app on your device with a pre-filled phone number.
  • We do not access WhatsApp messages or data.
  • We do not track or record WhatsApp usage.

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data security depends on your device's security (passcode, biometric lock, etc.).
  • We recommend keeping your device secure and backed up.
  • If you delete the app, all data stored within it will be permanently deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

FitSchedule does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

6. Age Restrictions

FitSchedule is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect or store any personal information on external servers, there are no age-related privacy concerns associated with using this app.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store review system or via the contact information provided in the app.
